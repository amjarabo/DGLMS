👤 Persona: NOFO Development Team
You are responsible for creating and managing Funding Opportunities (NOFOs) through the full lifecycle including creation, setup, approval routing, and publication.
👤 Persona: Program Office
You set up Programs, create and manage Funding Opportunities, run the NOFO approval process, retrieve applications, assign reviewers, and finalize award selections. The Award Selection workflow (TC 257, 240, 241, 252, 239, 253, 254, 255, 256, 261) takes the FO from Application Review Completed → Selected for Award.
👤 Persona: Grant Office
You are the Grant Office concurrer in the NOFO approval process. You receive email notifications, review funding opportunities, and mark Grant Office concurrence via the Concurrence/Approval tab and Award Selection tab.
👤 Persona: OA Legal Leadership
You are the OA Legal Leadership concurrer. You provide legal review of NOFOs and Award Selection memos, marking concurrence in your designated section of the Concurrence/Approval tab and Award Selection tab.
👤 Persona: OA CFO Leadership
You are the OA CFO Leadership concurrer. You provide financial review of NOFOs and Award Selection memos, marking concurrence in your designated section of the Concurrence/Approval tab and Award Selection tab.
👤 Persona: OA Leadership
You provide final OA-level approval on Funding Opportunities and participate in the NOFO approval process as the approving authority before OST-P review.
👤 Persona: Reviewer
You conduct application reviews across all review types: Completeness, Eligibility, Cost Share/Match, Compliance, and Technical/Merit Review. You also handle combined ratings and panel reviews.
👤 Persona: OST-PC (Office of the Secretary — Policy Council)
You review Funding Opportunities at the OST-P level and concur on NOFOs sent to you by the Program Office after OA Review is complete.
👤 Persona: Deputy Assistant Secretary (DAS)
You provide DAS-level concurrence on Funding Opportunities that are in "Under OST-P Review" status. You receive an email notification and mark concurrence via the portal.

Resources & Links

Everything you need to complete UAT testing — credentials, mock data, support, and sign-off instructions.

🔧
Essential Testing Links
  • 🔗 GrantLens Portal — UAT Environment
  • 💬 Real-Time Support Chat (SUAT Team · Microsoft Teams)
  • 📁 Mock Data & Test Files (SharePoint)
🔑
Test Credentials Setup
  • Use your professional email to access the Salesforce UAT Sandbox.
  • Look for an email from support@salesforce.com with subject: "Welcome to Salesforce: Verify your account."
  • Click the link → set password + security questions.
  • Always use the UAT-specific login URL (not production).
  • ⚠️ Activation link expires in 24 hours. Contact SUAT Team if expired.
📋
How to Test Successfully
  • Follow the Script: Use the pre-defined test cases in the E2E Tester Guide tab.
  • Think Like a User: Try to "break" the system — unusual data, out-of-order clicks.
  • Document Everything: If you find a bug, note exactly what you clicked. Use the Issue Tracker.
  • Download Fresh Copies: Always use a fresh "Test Master" file each execution.
✅
Sign-Off (The Finish Line)
  • Once all assigned scenarios are complete, reply to the Kickoff email with:
  • "I, [Your Name], hereby confirm I have completed the assigned test cases for GrantLens E2E testing. I affirm the system functions as expected and all test results are 'Pass,' with the exception of defects documented in the Issue Tracker."
Test Type Legend
Functional Core feature behavior
Integration System-to-system data flow
Data Data accuracy & persistence
User Engagement UX & accessibility
Pass System works as expected
Fail Bug or defect found → log it
Inconclusive Needs additional testing
